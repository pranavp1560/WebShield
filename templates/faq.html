A phishing website is a fake website designed to steal sensitive information such as usernames, passwords, and credit card details from unsuspecting users.

Phishing websites trick users into entering personal information by mimicking trusted websites.

Look for mismatched URLs, poor grammar, suspicious requests for sensitive data, and unsecure connections.

Use security software, double-check URLs, avoid clicking suspicious links, and never enter credentials on untrusted pages.

Tools like browser security extensions, antivirus software, and phishing detection websites can help you stay safe.

Immediately change your passwords, notify your bank if necessary, and report the phishing attempt to authorities or cybersecurity experts.
